FUN FACTS ABOUT ME

I'm learning to code

I love learning new things, and although a few years ago I gave this whole coding thing a shot, life took me on a different path. Though I was once hesitant to join the male-dominated STEM field, I’m excited to see it becoming more inclusive.
For my next steps in developing my skills, I am applying to a bootcamp. To support this, I have created this website and a Wordle game, which you can play here.

My name is Basque

I am from the Basque Country, a unique region in northern Spain (and part of France) with its own language.
And no, I don’t miss the heat—my hometown gets more rain than London!

My dream is to adopt a cat

I am a future cat-mum.
I already have a “niece-cat” and “nephew-cat” in Spain, and visiting them is always a highlight of my trips home.

I like Fish & Chips

After six years in London, I finally tried Fish & Chips.
It turns out, I like them—who knew!
